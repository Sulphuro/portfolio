Harbor seals inhabit temperate coastal waters and sport beautiful spotted coats.
These agile swimmers can dive deep to hunt for fish and other prey.
Noted for their expressive eyes, harbor seals captivate with their playful nature.
Adaptable and resilient, these seals thrive in a range of marine environments.
Seals communicate using a mix of sounds and body language to express themselves.
